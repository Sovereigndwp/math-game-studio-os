# Snack Line Shuffle — P1 Definition of Done

All items below must pass before advancing to P2A.

- Single screen cafeteria scene
- 2 to 4 children only
- addition within 5 only
- largest-first rule only
- per-placement adjacency glow uses neutral, green, amber only
- Serve tap is cosmetic only
- totals are never revealed
- amber feedback uses child voice line only, no numbers
- K to 1 difficulty remains within 10, addition dominant, 3 to 4 children maximum
- fantasy alignment teacher sign-off achieved from at least 2 K to 2 classroom teachers
- at least 70 percent of 6 to 10 K to 2 students can explain in their own words that they are ordering from most snacks to least snacks
- at least 60 percent spontaneously compute some sums verbally or with fingers rather than pure guessing
- Serve-spam rate is 20 percent of rounds or lower
- at least 50 percent continue voluntarily past the required demo with no extrinsic reward
- educator review confirms that the line ordering metaphor is clear and classroom-aligned
- educator review confirms that ordering direction is unambiguous for non-readers
- Grade 2 plus content gate is enforced and not present in K to 1 levels
- Game Family Registry is updated to show Snack Line Shuffle as first K to 2 member of Compare and Order
- 1.NBT.B.3 is removed from public-facing alignment claims
- Game Design Critic reviews the P1 pass record before P2A begins
