# Pre-Build Package: Power Grid Operator (High School)

## Game Experience Spec

**Core fantasy:** Keep a city online through the night by solving missing power needs and routing energy.
**Core action:** Read district demand and current supply, solve the missing value, send power from shared reserve, watch the district recover.
**Math domain:** Algebra — missing addend, missing factor, later multi-step equations and inequalities.
**Why not a worksheet:** The unknown has a real meaning — the missing power that keeps a district alive.
**Better-than-correct layer:** Use less waste, route more efficiently, keep more districts online later.
**Depth:** Very high. Correctness → efficiency → triage → optimization.
**Breadth:** Different districts, patterns, events, line states, reserve setups.
**Natural ceiling:** High if the city stays visually legible and the math remains central.

**Three memorable moments:**
1. You solve the last missing value and a whole block lights up
2. A hospital stabilizes just in time
3. Later, you keep more districts online than last run with less waste

**Stop condition:** If correct algebra still feels confusing or disconnected from the world, stop and rethink. If the screen feels like a dashboard instead of a living city, stop and address visual feel before adding mechanics.

**Current prototype status:** P1 (L1-L3) + P2A done. Algebra-first, no scarcity. Shared reserve visually legible.
