# Pre-Build Package: School Trip Fleet (Middle School)

## Game Experience Spec

**Core fantasy:** School district fleet coordinator getting student groups to field trips on time.
**Core action:** Read a group need, solve the missing capacity or ratio, assign the best bus or bus mix, watch the trip depart.
**Math domain:** Division, multiplication, ceiling division, ratio, optimization.
**Why not a worksheet:** The ratio directly determines whether the trip works. Wrong capacity = students stranded.
**Better-than-correct layer:** Minimize wasted seats, fuel, and missed destination windows.
**Depth:** Correct assignment → efficient assignment → schedule prioritization.
**Breadth:** Many bus mixes, group sizes, route timings, destination types.
**Natural ceiling:** High for middle school if capacity efficiency becomes meaningful.

**Three memorable moments:**
1. Save a museum visit with the last workable bus
2. Realize a smaller bus + van wastes fewer seats than one large bus
3. Two groups seem impossible until you reorder the departures

**Stop condition:** If the game becomes only matching group size to the only bus that fits, stop and redesign.
