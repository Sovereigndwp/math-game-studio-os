# Pre-Build Package: Firefly Ranger (Elementary)

## Game Experience Spec

**Core fantasy:** Night ranger bringing light back to a dark forest.
**Core action:** Read how much glow a place needs, choose the right jars, carry the light there, watch the place come alive.
**Math domain:** Addition, counting, early number combinations.
**Why not a worksheet:** The number is the exact light needed to restore a real place. The world changes visibly.
**Better-than-correct layer:** Use fewer jars. Waste less glow. Choose the smartest order.
**Depth:** Correct totals → efficiency → route planning.
**Breadth:** Forest zones, changing needs, changing paths, different jar sets.
**Natural ceiling:** Moderate to high for elementary if efficiency and route choice are added carefully.

**Three memorable moments:**
1. A raccoon burrow lights up just before the moon drops
2. A whole row of lanterns turns on in sequence
3. You save three places with exactly the right jars and no wasted glow

**Stop condition:** If the world reaction is too weak or the choice feels like plain addition, stop and rethink.
