# K-12 Subject & Skill Registry

# 🔢 Mathematics

## K–2 Number Sense

- Counting to 120
- Addition & subtraction within 20
- Place value to 100

## 3–5 Operations

- Multiplication & division fluency
- Fractions — equivalence and ordering
- Multi-digit arithmetic

## 6–8 Algebra & Geometry

- Ratios and proportional reasoning
- Linear equations and inequalities
- Geometry: area, volume, transformations

## 9–12 Advanced Math

- Trigonometry and the unit circle
- Statistics and probability
- Calculus: limits, derivatives, integrals

# 📖 English / ELA

- Phonics and decoding (K–2)
- Reading comprehension strategies (3–8)
- Vocabulary and morphology (3–12)
- Grammar and mechanics (K–8)

# 🔬 Science

- Life cycles and ecosystems (K–5)
- Forces, motion, and energy (3–8)
- Chemistry: matter, reactions (6–12)
- Earth and space science (K–12)

# 💻 Coding / CS

- Sequencing and loops (K–5)
- Conditionals and functions (3–8)
- Data structures and algorithms (6–12)
