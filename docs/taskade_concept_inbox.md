# Taskade Concept Inbox

## Purpose

This document lists game concepts that are **tracked in Taskade but do not yet have a formal concept packet, a review build, or a family-registry entry in this repo**. It is a **staging inbox**, not a validation list. Inclusion here does NOT imply that any of these concepts has:

- an approved CCSS alignment in this repo,
- a formal grade-band lock,
- a `concepts/<slug>/` packet,
- a `reviews/<slug>/` review build,
- pass records,
- or membership in [`memory/registries/family_registry.json`](../memory/registries/family_registry.json).

It means only that the concept exists as a row, card, or narrative entry in one of the Taskade projects preserved under [`taskade_exports/projects/`](../taskade_exports/projects/), and that someone — eventually — needs to decide whether to packetize it, keep it in the inbox, or retire it.

All Taskade-origin CCSS, grade-band, and ceiling claims reproduced here are **as-claimed in Taskade**, not as-validated in the repo. They are written in italic voice (*"Taskade claims …"*) to keep that provenance explicit. Do not treat any of these claims as a repo source of truth.

If a concept in this inbox is later given a formal repo packet, remove it from here and add it to its proper durable location instead.
