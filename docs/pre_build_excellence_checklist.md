# Pre-Build Excellence Checklist

A quick honest gut check before formal evaluation. Use this after the Game Experience Spec draft and before the Delight Gate scoring. If a concept cannot answer most of these clearly, it is not ready for implementation.

## Use this when
- A new concept has a spec draft but hasn't been formally scored yet
- You want to catch weak concepts before investing in detailed evaluation
- You suspect a concept is functional but not wonderful

## Do not use this for
- Replacing the Game Experience Spec (which requires detailed answers)
- Replacing the Delight Gate (which formally scores pass/fail)
- Evaluating existing games mid-pass

---

## Checklist

### 1. Why would the player care?
What feels at stake? What would success or failure feel like?

### 2. What 3 moments could be memorable?
Name 3 moments a player might later retell.

### 3. What is the first mastery layer beyond correctness?
Once the player can get the right answer, what makes "doing it well" different?

### 4. How much depth and breadth does the concept have?
Can the loop deepen, and can replay create genuinely different problems?

### 5. Is the world model legible at a glance?
Can the player understand what is connected, shared, limited, or changing before reading a long explanation?

### 6. Are the decisions real?
Which choices produce meaningfully different outcomes, and which are fake or decorative?

### 7. What accidental friction could make this feel bad?
What might make the game harder for the wrong reason?

### 8. What would prove this concept is weak enough to stop early?
What specific failure would tell us not to keep polishing it?

### 9. Why is this not a worksheet in costume?
How does the role and world change the meaning of the math?

### 10. What is the natural ceiling?
After how many sessions would a player feel they have seen everything important?

---

## How to use the answers

- If 7+ questions have clear, specific answers → proceed to Delight Gate
- If 4-6 questions have clear answers → strengthen the spec before scoring
- If fewer than 4 → the concept needs fundamental rethinking, not more detail

---

*This checklist sits between the Game Experience Spec (detailed design) and the Delight Gate (formal scoring). It is the "is this actually worth building?" moment.*
